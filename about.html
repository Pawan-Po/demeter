Demeter is known to be the goddess of harvest, in relation to that our project ‘The Demeter’ focuses on elevating the agriculture sector of Bhutan as agriculture is said to the backbone of our country. Moreover, agriculture hold an important role in our country as it is considered to be one of the jewels for the economic developments and also the agriculture based society contributes over 16% of the country’s GDP (Gross Domestic Product), as per Labour force survey.
Over the course of time the agricultural practices in Bhutan have changed tremendously and have brought upon huge improvement in the lives of farmers and their yield but at times the farmers in the rural areas tend to go without the proper agricultural practices to yield their harvest. So our main objective is to reach out to the farmers in the rural area and mould their knowledge on the right agricultural practices that would help the environment as well as boost their yield.
